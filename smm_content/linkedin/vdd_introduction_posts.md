## Post 5: The Human-in-the-Loop Principle

```
VDD's core insight: You're not managing code, you're managing an AI's understanding of code.

HILing (Human-in-the-Loop-ing) means:
- Review AI's interpretation before implementation
- Correct misunderstandings early
- Guide, don't dictate

It's like conducting an orchestra - you don't play every instrument, but you ensure they play in harmony.

#HumanInTheLoop #VDD #AICollaboration
```
